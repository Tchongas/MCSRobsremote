Appearance

ROBS lets you adjust the interface so it fits your screen and your workflow. You can scale things up or down, hide panels you do not use, and turn on a few comfort options.

Visibility

You can hide the parts of the app you do not need. Turn off the console, stream controls, dashboard, scene list, or quick actions. There is also a plugin-only mode that hides everything except the plugin sidebar, which is handy on small screens or when you only want the buttons.

High Contrast

High contrast mode makes the colors stronger and the interface easier to read.
